## Page Composition Example

A typical landing page composes these patterns in this order:

1. **Hero Section** вҖ” warm cream background
2. **Brand Ticker** вҖ” dark strip
3. **Services Cards** вҖ” white background
4. **Image + Text Split** (image left) вҖ” white
5. **Value Props** вҖ” cream background
6. **Image Mosaic** вҖ” full-bleed
7. **Image + Text Split** (text left, with checklist) вҖ” cream
8. **Anti-Aging Feature** вҖ” light cream
9. **FAQ Accordion** вҖ” white
10. **Testimonials** вҖ” cream
11. **CTA Banner** вҖ” warm beige
12. **Footer** вҖ” dark

### Background Alternation Pattern

Sections alternate between warm cream (`--warm-200`) and near-white (`--warm-50`) to create visual rhythm without hard dividers. Dark sections (ticker, footer) provide contrast anchors.

